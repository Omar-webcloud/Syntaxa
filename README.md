# Syntaxa

**Live Link**: [https://syntaxa-ten.vercel.app/](https://syntaxa-ten.vercel.app/)

## Features
- **Daily Quizzes**: Keep your grammar sharp with daily English quizzes.
- **Dictionary**: Search for English words and get their Bengali meanings, synonyms, and pronunciation.
- **Practice Mode**: Improve your sentence structure and correct common errors.
- **Progress Tracking**: Track your streaks, total quizzes, and average accuracy.
- **Rewards**: Earn gems and trophies as you learn and complete goals!
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Toggle the theme exactly to your preference.
